    Antoine Aylwin

    Antoine Aylwin is a Quebecois lawyer known for his expertise in business law and corporate governance. He has also been involved in causes related to human rights and social justice. Aylwin is often perceived as an enlightened leader engaged in his community, using his voice and legal skills to promote progress and equity.
